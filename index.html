About me

I moved to the United States in 2001 with my family and lived in Georgia ever since. I attended college in Georgia State University for 2 years as a Computer Science major. I have been married since March of 2015, currently have no kids but I have 2 pets, a dog named Mandoo and a Cat named SuzyQ. Currently I work for as the Senior Director and Branch Manager for an appliance repair company. Our company has many contracts with many different vendors, manufacturers and warranty companies. My hobbies include eating tasty foods, watching anime and playing a lot of games of every type. I stream myself playing video games on Twitch.
